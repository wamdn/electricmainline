Pleased to Meet You

Leigh Whipday doesn't like writing in the third person, so that's enough of that. Instead I'll tell you that in the last 15 years, I've worked with great agencies and exciting brands from all over the world. More importantly, I've worked alongside many talented people who have taught me a lot along the way. I've been lucky enough to live and work in New York, where I was an Art Director/Associate Creative Director at Firstborn.

Now as a freelancer, I work remotely from sunny Manchester, England. I don't believe 'exposure' is a form of currency. I do believe in taking the correct approach to answer the brief, regardless of trends. I'm polite and professional and genuinely care about the work I do. If we were to work together, I'd hope for the same from you. You can also read interviews I did with The FWA and EP.
